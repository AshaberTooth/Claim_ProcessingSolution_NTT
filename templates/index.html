Claims Agentic AI MVP

This prototype demonstrates an Azure AI Foundry workflow for healthcare claim processing. It extracts claim data from uploaded documents, runs multiple AI agents, applies simple eligibility and adjudication logic, and produces a final claim recommendation.

What it does

Processes claim PDFs or images using Document Intelligence and Azure AI Foundry agents.

Expected input

A claim PDF/image containing claim ID, member ID, provider ID, date of service, diagnosis code, procedure code, and billed amount.

Expected output

Structured claim extraction, eligibility result, adjudication recommendation, policy evidence, and final decision.
